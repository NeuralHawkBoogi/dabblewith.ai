Community-bot validation · price probe

Turn a good group-owner call into a clean willingness-to-pay signal.

Use this only after a real Casagrand group owner/admin has pain and pilot interest. The goal is not to announce pricing publicly; it is to learn whether the Get a Community Bot product has budget, urgency, and a sellable package.

gate

Ask after value

Do not lead with price. Ask only after the owner confirms repeated questions, event/RSVP cadence, or admin-summary pain.

measure

Capture bands

Log only willingness bands: no, maybe, yes; then optional range: none, under ₹2k, ₹2k–₹5k, ₹5k+.

decision

Route next action

Strong pain plus yes/maybe WTP moves to design-partner call. Low pain or no WTP parks the lead without custom work.

Soft willingness probe

One quick check so I do not overbuild this: if a WhatsApp community bot saved you admin time by answering repeated FAQs, collecting RSVPs/interests, and sending a daily summary, would this be something your group/business/resident community could pay for after a working pilot? You can just reply: no / maybe / yes. If maybe or yes, what range feels reasonable after proof: under ₹2k/month, ₹2k–₹5k/month, or ₹5k+/month?

Package probe

For the pilot, assume a simple package: - setup the bot voice, rules, FAQs, and escalation - collect event/interests from members - daily admin summary - one weekly improvement pass If this works for 7 days, which package would make sense to test next? A) free/community experiment only B) starter monthly plan after proof C) paid setup + monthly plan D) not useful for this group
