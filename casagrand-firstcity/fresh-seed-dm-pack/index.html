QA/dev/student DM

Hey — I’m testing a small “AI by Doing” circle for Casagrand residents. Since you’re into tech/QA/dev/student projects, I’m looking for one real task to turn into a tiny AI workflow. Could you share one small thing you’d actually use? Example: turn a feature note into test cases, explain a bug, prep for an interview, or plan a mini project. No sensitive company data — even a fake/sanitized example is fine. If it works, I’ll show you the workflow and ask for one resident intro who has a similar problem.

Excel/workflow DM

Hey — I’m testing a practical AI workflow circle for Casagrand residents. I’m looking for one everyday office task where AI can save time. Do you have a small task like cleaning an Excel sheet, summarizing notes, drafting a report/email, or turning messy data into an action list? Please don’t send private company data. A fake/sanitized sample is perfect. If it’s useful, I’ll share the workflow back and ask for one resident intro with a similar task.

Founder/freelancer DM

Hey — I’m testing a practical AI builders circle for Casagrand residents who run side projects, consulting, freelancing, or small businesses. I’m looking for one repetitive business task to convert into an AI workflow: proposal draft, lead research, follow-up message, landing page critique, competitor summary, invoice/admin checklist, etc. If you can share a fake/sanitized example, I’ll turn it into a tiny workflow and send it back. If it helps, I’ll ask for one intro to another resident founder/freelancer.

Group-owner/admin DM

Hey — I’m exploring whether a lightweight WhatsApp community helper would be useful for Casagrand groups. Not asking to add a bot anywhere. Just trying to understand the repeated admin pain: FAQs, event RSVPs, reminders, member onboarding, issue routing, summaries, or polls. If you manage or help with any group, can you share the one most repetitive coordination task? I’ll keep it private and only use it to design a small demo.
