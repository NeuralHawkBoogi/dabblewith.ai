how to use

Run it once, then improve it.

Copy the worksheet into Notion, Google Docs, Linear, or a plain text file. Fill it with sanitized examples only. After the first run, update the failure notes before sharing it with anyone else.

Human gate: An organizer must read every intake reply before clustering. LLM may suggest themes but cannot decide who attends or what is exposed.

Privacy: Do not paste real customer messages, health, or financial data into the LLM. Use sanitized examples or schemas only.
