Related: homepage first-5 outreach, warm WhatsApp replies, community-bot validation

## Why this exists

The first warm homepage-forward batch produced 2 meaningful WhatsApp responders out of 5. That is enough signal to avoid a broad blast and instead convert the first responders into one concrete builder session/call.

Use the mobile kit here:

- `https://dabblewith.ai/homepage-outreach/builder-session/`

## Current hypothesis

Warm homepage traffic converts when the ask is specific:

> Send your role, one workflow/task you want AI help with, and the format you prefer.

The strongest first-batch topic is **agentic workflows / developer productivity / practical AI learning**. The next proof should be a single focused builder discussion or mini-session, not a generic community announcement.

## Operator sequence

1. Send the builder-call invite to the deeper builder responder.
2. Send the mini-session invite to the agentic workflow / developer responder.
3. If either person mentions owning or managing a group, send the community-bot probe and route them to `/community-bot/`.
4. Wait for one concrete workflow sample or preferred stack/sample task.
5. Only after one call/session is booked, send the next 3–5 warm DMs using the same homepage CTA.

## Session shape

Working title: **Build a safe CLI/file-system memory agent**

- 5 min: pick one real task and define boundaries.
- 10 min: show the loop — read context, plan, act, verify, log memory.
- 10 min: map the responder’s stack and constraints.
- 10 min: decide next proof — mini-demo, office-hours topic, or community-bot interview.

Success metric: one concrete workflow sample and one reusable learning/session title.

## Tracking fields

Keep this privacy-safe:

- Outreach batch: first5 or next5
- Responder id: redacted last four only
- Stage: builder-call-invited / sample-requested / mini-session-invited / community-bot-probed / booked / parked
- Topic: agentic workflows, developer workflow, AI learning, community-bot, other
- Next action date

Never store full phone numbers, raw WhatsApp payloads, message IDs, tokens, or private message transcripts in public repo artifacts.

## Decision thresholds

- 0 concrete workflow samples after follow-up: rewrite the homepage forward around a more specific promise.
- 1 concrete workflow sample: book a 20-minute builder discussion and turn it into a reusable mini-session outline.
- 2+ concrete workflow samples: schedule a small live/WhatsApp walkthrough before a broader Casagrand IT-group post.
- Any group-owner/admin pain: prioritize community-bot readiness and design-partner interview.
