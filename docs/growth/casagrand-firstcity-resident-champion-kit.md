# Casagrand First City Resident Champion Kit

Date: 2026-05-22  
Purpose: recruit three trusted resident champions before the first broad Casagrand First City IT-group launch post.

## Why this exists

The campaign already has launch copy, RSVP/date pages, first-10 tester DMs, admin permission copy, and office-hours loops. The remaining growth risk is social proof: a first post into a ~600-member group can feel cold if nobody trusted replies quickly.

The champion layer makes the first public post feel resident-led instead of brand-led.

## Champion target

Recruit exactly three warm residents before the broad post:

1. **Career champion** — job search, resume, interview prep, IT opportunities group relevance.
2. **Workflow/founder champion** — office automation, founder/freelancer, reporting, email, research, sales/admin use cases.
3. **Community-bot champion** — club, tutor, founder group, resident initiative, association-adjacent, or group admin who may validate the WhatsApp community-bot product.

## Success threshold

Proceed to broad launch when at least two of these are true:

- 3 champions agree to help.
- 6+ real use cases are collected.
- 3 seeded replies/votes are committed for the first public post.
- 1–2 community-bot referrals are identified.

If fewer than two champions agree within 24 hours, use `/casagrand-firstcity/testers/` and run the first-10 tester sprint before posting broadly.

## Decision rule

- 3 champions + 6 use cases → broad launch from `/casagrand-firstcity/launch-board/`.
- 2 champions + strong community-bot referral → run design-partner call before broad event push.
- 0–1 champions → private first-10 tester sprint first.

- Do not frame the resident helper bot as official Casagrand software unless formally approved.
