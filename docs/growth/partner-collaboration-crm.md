# Dabblewith.ai Partner Collaboration / Issue-Swap CRM

Status: active
Owner: Boogi / dabblewith.ai
Related tickets: `WEB-GROWTH-T10`
Last updated: 2026-05-26

## Goal

Maintain a lightweight, human-readable shortlist of newsletter authors, community runners, and AI-builder publishers we want to do issue swaps, guest posts, or Substack Live conversations with — without storing private contact data or anything that could be considered surveillance of an audience.

Pairs with `docs/growth/audience-acquisition-community-strategy.md` (channel strategy) and `docs/growth/utm-link-conventions.md` (so every collaboration is trackable).

## Privacy rules

- Do not store anyone's private phone number or unlisted email here.
- Use the partner's public contact channel only — their newsletter reply address, public DM handle, or a link to their contact page.
- Do not store anyone's audience members. We track *partners*, not their readers.
- If a partner asks to be removed from this list, remove them in the same working day.

## Outreach principles

1. Practical AI / builder fit, not "anyone with subscribers".
2. Reach out with a specific reason (a post they wrote, a workflow we can fork).
3. Lead with what we'll give first — usually a guest workflow, not a pitch.
4. Honest disclosure on both sides for any swap, sponsorship, or mention.
5. Never run a paid promotion disguised as editorial.

## Suggested categories

| Category | Why | Fit signals |
|---|---|---|
| Practical AI for founders/operators | Direct overlap with our top workflow categories. | Talks about real workflows, not just tool hype. |
| Indie / build-in-public newsletters | Audience already values honest weekly updates. | Posts numbers, mentions failures, links to other builders. |
| Community / WhatsApp / Discord ops | Direct overlap with the community bot. | Writes about moderation, onboarding, retention. |
| AI for researchers / academics | Long-tail workflow demand (paper summarization, lit review). | Talks about citation-grounded LLM use. |
| AI for healthcare / clinical / safety-critical ops | Audience values "what to never automate" framing. | Discusses HIPAA/PHI, clinical safety, human-in-the-loop. |
| Creator / writing / voice AI | Aligned with creator content repurposing workflow. | Talks about voice preservation, not just generation. |
| No-code / automation / lightweight ops | Aligned with no-code lead qualification workflow. | Builds workflows people can fork in an afternoon. |

## Outreach copy templates

>
> Hi NAME,
>
> I read your post on TOPIC and want to ship one back at you, not just compliment it. I run Dabblewith.ai, a small AI Builder's Exchange where we publish forkable workflows with explicit human-review points.
>
> Would you be open to an issue swap? I'll write a guest workflow for your audience — your pick of topic from our [workflow exchange](https://dabblewith.ai/workflows/) — and you can write one for ours. Honest disclosure on both sides. No paid placement.
>
> If yes, I'll send a draft within a week.
>
> Boogi
> dabblewith.ai

>
> Boogi

### Template C — Cold-warm partner DM

> Hi NAME — short note. I'm Boogi from Dabblewith.ai (small AI Builder's Exchange). Saw your work on TOPIC. I'd love to send you one of our workflows on RELEVANT_AUDIENCE and hear what you'd change — not asking you to subscribe, just want your take. Reply here if open.

## How rows move

`open` → `contacted` (date sent) → `responded` (date) → `agreed` → `published` → `archived`.

If `contacted` and no response in 14 days, set back to `open` with a date stamp. Do not re-pitch the same partner more than twice without a new reason.

## Don't list

- Anyone who has previously asked us not to contact them.
- Anyone whose primary audience overlaps with regulated/protected populations where we'd be at risk of inappropriate cross-promotion (e.g., specific patient communities).
- Anyone who's purely promoting affiliate stacks rather than practical workflows.
