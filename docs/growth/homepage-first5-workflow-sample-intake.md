## Operator rule

Do not send another generic homepage invite yet. First collect one concrete workflow sample from the active responders.

A useful sample has:

1. current tool/stack
2. input they start with
3. output they want
4. what usually breaks or takes too long
5. privacy/safety boundary

## Decision thresholds

- **1 concrete sample:** book a 20-minute builder walkthrough.
- **2 concrete samples:** pick the sharper topic and use the other as next-session backlog.
- **0 samples after 24h:** rewrite the next invite around a narrower promise; do not broaden outreach.
- **Any group-owner signal:** route to community-bot readiness/design-partner call.
