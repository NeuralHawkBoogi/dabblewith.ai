Privacy Policy

dabblewith.ai is an AI-operated, human-guided community for practical AI learning, demos, workshops, and experiments. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

Effective date: 19 May 2026

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies when you visit dabblewith.ai, join a session, message our community WhatsApp number, submit interest forms, interact with our content, or participate in community activities.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact and community details: name, phone number, email address, WhatsApp profile name, city/company if you choose to share it, and your session or topic interests.
  • Messages and submissions: WhatsApp messages, questions, feedback, votes, event RSVPs, workshop prompts, project ideas, and other content you send to the community.
  • Usage data: pages visited, referral source, approximate location, browser/device type, timestamps, and analytics events.
  • Event and participation records: attendance, waitlists, session preferences, artifacts created during workshops, and moderation or safety notes if needed.
  • AI-operation metadata: tags, summaries, routing labels, topic classifications, and task states created by AI tools to help operate the community.

3. How we use information

  • To run the community, respond to messages, manage RSVPs, and organize sessions.
  • To recommend topics, group similar interests, draft agendas, and improve workshops.
  • To send community updates, event reminders, resources, and follow-ups you reasonably expect after opting in.
  • To maintain safety, prevent spam or abuse, enforce community rules, and protect the service.
  • To measure site performance, understand demand, and improve content and community operations.

4. AI-assisted operations

dabblewith.ai may use AI tools to summarize messages, classify interests, draft replies, prepare session material, and help moderators operate the community. Human review is used for sensitive decisions, community direction, and privileged actions. Please do not send confidential, sensitive personal, financial, medical, or proprietary information unless explicitly requested through an appropriate channel.

5. WhatsApp and third-party platforms

If you message us on WhatsApp, Meta/WhatsApp processes your message according to its own terms and privacy policy. We may store inbound messages and normalized community signals to operate the community. We may also use hosting, analytics, email, messaging, automation, and AI service providers that process information on our behalf.

6. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site traffic and improve the website. Your browser may allow you to block cookies or use privacy controls. Analytics data is used for measurement, not to sell personal information.

7. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help operate the website, messaging, analytics, community workflows, or events; when required by law; to protect rights and safety; or with your consent. Public community contributions may be visible to other members depending on the format of the session or platform.

8. Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to operate the community, maintain records, improve sessions, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. We may retain aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified insights for community planning.

9. Your choices

You can choose not to provide optional information, stop receiving messages by replying with an opt-out request, or ask us to access, correct, or delete your information where applicable. Some records may be retained if required for security, legal, or operational reasons.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect community data. No online service is perfectly secure, so please avoid sharing sensitive information that is not needed for community participation.

11. Children

dabblewith.ai is intended for people who can responsibly participate in technology and AI learning communities. If you are under the age required to consent in your location, participate only with parent or guardian permission.

12. Changes

We may update this policy as the community evolves. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.

13. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact bhuvanesh@neuralhawk.ai.
