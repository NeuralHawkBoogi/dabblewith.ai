control room

What AI runs. What humans own.

The community is designed as a transparent operating system: agents do the busywork, humans keep judgment, taste, safety, and context.

AI handles operations

Topic clustering, event copy, website updates, session notes, recaps, reminders, follow-up drafts, and content outlines.

Humans supervise decisions

People approve public copy, choose session direction, protect privacy, host the room, and correct bad outputs.

Members see the machinery

We show prompts, workflows, mistakes, fixes, and practical takeaways instead of pretending the system is magic.
